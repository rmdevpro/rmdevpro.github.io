MIT Data Science Capstone

I completed the MIT Professional Education Applied Data Science Program in October 2025. The program covered Python foundations, statistics, exploratory data analysis and visualization, network analysis, unsupervised learning, supervised learning (regression and classification), model evaluation (cross-validation and bootstrapping), decision trees, random forests, time series analysis, neural networks, convolutional neural networks, transformers, recommendation systems, and generative AI. The capstone project applied customer segmentation techniques to a marketing campaign dataset.
