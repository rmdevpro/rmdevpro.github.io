Nearly 30 years in tech — infrastructure, systems architecture, software engineering — mostly in startups where you build what you need and make it work. In mid-2025 I turned that experience toward AI and started building.

**Joshua26** is the result: a distributed multi-agent ecosystem running across three bare-metal hosts, with a dozen autonomous services that build, test, and maintain software with minimal human intervention. It's not a demo. It runs in production every day.

Along the way I developed an original framework for how agentic systems should be structured — 24 concept papers covering everything from agent identity and autonomy to multi-model collaboration to why conversation itself is the right substrate for agent cognition. I hold an MIT certification in Generative AI.

I'm currently looking for senior leadership roles where I can bring this thinking to bear at scale.
