Today I am releasing the first of 23 concept papers on Conversationally Cognizant AI — a framework for thinking about agentic systems that emerged from building two successive AI agent ecosystems.

Paper 1: Conversationally Cognizant AI Systems

This is the foundational paper. It defines ten primitives — Conversation, Context, Inference, Identity, Purpose, Domain, Intent, Persona, Autonomy, and Metacognition — and seven principles that follow from incorporating them into design.

These are practitioner's hypotheses, not academic claims. They came from building, not from literature review. Where they converge with established work, that convergence is independent — I encountered the problems and reasoned toward solutions from first principles.
