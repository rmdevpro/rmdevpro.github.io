Tech Executive & Hands-on AI Researcher

Nearly 30 years leading and transforming technology organizations — from governing 2,000+ applications across a global enterprise to founding and capitalizing a $9.4M venture — and in mid-2025 I turned all of that toward AI and started learning, thinking, and building.

Joshua26 is the result: a distributed multi-agent ecosystem spanning three bare-metal hosts and a dozen cloud services, with autonomous agents that build, test, and maintain software with minimal human intervention. It's not a demo. It runs in production every day.

Along the way I developed Conversationally Cognizant AI — an original framework for how agentic systems should be structured — 23 concept papers covering everything from agent identity and autonomy to multi-model collaboration to why conversation itself is the right substrate for agent cognition. I hold an MIT certification in Generative AI as well as masters degrees in both IT and Business Administration.

I'm currently looking for senior leadership roles where I can bring my skills, experience and approach to an innovation focussed organization.
