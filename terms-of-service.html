Joshua26 System Alerting Service

This service sends automated SMS notifications from the Joshua26 AI research infrastructure to the system administrator. By providing your phone number for system alerts, you agree to these terms.

Program Description

Program name: Joshua26 System Alerts

Purpose: Automated delivery of infrastructure monitoring alerts including system health status, pipeline errors, extraction progress, and scheduled reports.

Message frequency: Varies based on system events. Typically fewer than 10 messages per day under normal operation. Higher volume during system incidents.

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-In and Opt-Out

The system administrator opts in by configuring their phone number in the alerter configuration. This is a manual, deliberate configuration step — not a public sign-up.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any alert message. You can also remove your phone number from the alerter configuration at any time.

For help: Reply HELP to any alert message, or contact j@4morr.com.
